Start for free with lynda.com

卡西莫多cc
2019-01-11
General
Learn

Lynda.com is an massive open online course website,offering video courses taught by industry experts in software,creative,and bussiness skills,be acquired by LinkedIn in 2015,but unfortunately,Lynda dose not offer free service in China.
so I will tell you how to get Lynda’s Premium account for free,if you are student and not have enough money to get it.

First,you need to get a houston address,visit this website to create houston account.
Now you need to register in houston library,so you can get a houston library card number.with the help of detail that you got from previous step fill up the register form.
When you have done,you will get a temporary user id,note that down.
Next,move to lynda.com,sign in with you organization,put houstonlibrary.org in the field.and create a new profile,then you will be asked for library card number(your temporary user id),and put you new password just fill what you want.
Finally,lynda will ask you that whether you have any old account or not,just click on ‘no,I don’t have any’.you will be asked for an email address. Put your email address there and Done!
Congratulations, you successfully created premium lynda’s account for free.
